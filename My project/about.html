About Bubaha's Farm

A family-run farm dedicated to raising healthy, well-bred rams with transparent care and support for local farmers.

Our Story

Bubaha's Farm started as a small family project with a straightforward goal: to breed robust, reliable livestock suited to our local climate and farming systems. Over the years we've grown our pastures, refined our breeding choices, and invested in on‑farm veterinary partnerships so customers can trust the animals they buy from us.

We balance traditional husbandry knowledge with modern herd-health practices — selecting breeding stock not only for appearance but for structural correctness, temperament, and functional performance. Our animals are raised on pasture, supplemented when necessary to support growth and breeding demands.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to support resilient local farming by providing quality rams that improve flock performance and farm profitability. We operate with transparency, offering clear health records and pedigree information where available. We value animal welfare, responsible breeding, and long‑term customer relationships.

Breeding Program

Rams are chosen for their conformation, temperament, and proven ability to sire healthy offspring. Each candidate goes through a selection process that includes structural assessment, temperament observation, and health screening. We track lineage and performance traits to make incremental genetic improvements across seasons.

Health & Biosecurity

Maintaining herd health is a top priority. We work with a local veterinarian to set vaccination schedules (including CDT where appropriate), perform routine parasite control, and implement hoof and dental care. Biosecurity measures are in place for visitors to prevent disease introduction; please arrange visits by appointment.

What We Offer

Healthy Rams

Hand‑raised, vet‑checked rams suitable for breeding and meat production. We provide health documentation with each sale and advice on transport and acclimation.

Farm Visits

Inspect animals in person by appointment. We welcome buyers to see stock, review records, and discuss breeding goals before purchase.

After‑Sale Support

Practical guidance on initial feeding, housing, and integrating new rams into your flock. We aim to make the transition smooth and successful.

Meet The Team

We are a small team of experienced stockmen and a veterinary partner. Our daily work covers breeding decisions, pasture management, animal handling, and record keeping. We prioritise hands‑on welfare checks and provide clear communication to buyers.

Sustainability & Practice

We manage pastures with rotational grazing to protect soil and maintain forage quality. Supplementary feeding targets periods of high demand (pregnancy, lactation, and finishing). Our approach focuses on long‑term land health and animal productivity.
