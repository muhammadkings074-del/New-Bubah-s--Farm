Quality, Transparency, Care

At Bubaha's Farm we specialise in breeding rams suited for both breeding programmes and market sale. We focus on selecting animals with strong structural correctness, calm temperament, and consistent performance. Our production system is pasture-based with supplementary nutrition during critical growth and breeding stages.

Below are some of the ways we ensure quality and value for our customers.

Responsible Breeding

We select sires and dams based on conformation, temperament, and health records to improve flock resilience and growth rates over generations.

Health First

All animals receive routine vaccinations, parasite control, and regular veterinary checks; health records are provided at sale.

Buyer Support

We offer guidance on transport, acclimation, and feeding the first weeks after purchase to help new owners get the best start.
